Me, Myself and I

All the way from the home of champions, Bashir came to the city in search of an engineering degree but ended up falling in love with comedy. Bashir is a writer, stand-up comedian and MC. Known for his witty and smart style of writing halaiki has made a name for himself by talking about his experience as a broke university student trying to survive in a city known for its character development. The first rising star first made waves in the industry after being named among the top ten best standup comedians in kenya and since then has lived up and exceeded everyones expectations. His first major breakthrough came when he was hosted by Spice Fm in their inaugural show dubbed "what's so funny?" which was meant to showcase upcoming comedians in kenya. His time on air did not end there after he went ahead and got his TV debut later on when he was hosted by MC Jessy on KTN. Halaiki as he is porpularly know has performed in and hosted various shows across the nation including Saturday Night Comedy, Nairobi International Comedy Festival, Spare My Ribs and various shows for Punchline Comedy Club. Bashir has also showcased his MCEE skills by hosting everything from a wine tasting to a graduation party and most recently he was the on screen host for the Commonwealth Esports championship Kenyan qualifiers. He has also hosted two live shows for the biggest podcast in kenya The Sandwich Podcast. Bashir recently made his international debut by performing alongside comedy legend Bob Zany on the international comedy night which features top acts from across the world. Still young in the game but with an already impressive track record it's interesting to see how far this young star will go.

For over a decade I had many opportunities to work in a vast spectrum of web technologies what let me gather a significant amount of various experience. Working for companies and individuals around the globe I met and learnt from amazing and ambitious people.

I currently work remotely with a selected freelance client base being open for new opportunities.
